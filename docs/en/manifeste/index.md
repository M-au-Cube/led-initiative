## Manifesto content

The LED manifesto calls for:

- **Liberty** that is responsible, framed by ethical and environmental limits
- A demanding **ecological policy** and drastic reduction of consumption
- **Democracy** renewed through the Sixth Republic and digital referendums
- French and European **sovereignty** facing 21st century challenges
