# LED Timeline

Indicative roadmap for the **LED** initiative — from foundation to program implementation after the **June 2027 presidential election**.

!!! note "Projection document"
    This timeline is an **illustrative projection** for educational purposes. Dates and milestones may be adjusted according to the actual political calendar.

<div class="led-timeline" markdown="0">

<div class="led-timeline-item led-timeline--prep">
  <time>November 2025</time>
  <h3>Launch of the LED initiative</h3>
  <p>Publication of the manifesto and launch of <strong>led-initiative.fr</strong>. First citizen signature campaign.</p>
</div>

<div class="led-timeline-item led-timeline--prep">
  <time>January 2026</time>
  <h3>Founding convention</h3>
  <p>Official adoption of the three pillars — Liberty, Environment, Democracy — and validation of the 2027 program draft.</p>
</div>

<div class="led-timeline-item led-timeline--prep">
  <time>March 2026</time>
  <h3>Initiative registration</h3>
  <p>Filing of statutes and administrative approval. Establishment of local committees in pilot regions.</p>
</div>

<div class="led-timeline-item led-timeline--prep">
  <time>June 2026</time>
  <h3>Internal budget referendum</h3>
  <p>Digital consultation of members on public spending priorities: debt reduction, defense, energy transition.</p>
</div>

<div class="led-timeline-item led-timeline--prep">
  <time>September 2026</time>
  <h3>Pilot Obelix Feasts</h3>
  <p>First municipal editions of the "Obelix Feasts" to test the national cohesion program.</p>
</div>

<div class="led-timeline-item led-timeline--prep">
  <time>December 2026</time>
  <h3>Blue-White-Red Card presentation</h3>
  <p>Unveiling of the card project guaranteeing housing, health, food and hygiene — foundation of the future social reform.</p>
</div>

<div class="led-timeline-item led-timeline--prep">
  <time>April 2027</time>
  <h3>Presidential campaign launch</h3>
  <p>Official launch of the LED candidacy. National tour on digital sovereignty and French production.</p>
</div>

<div class="led-timeline-item led-timeline--election">
  <time>June 2027</time>
  <h3>Presidential election</h3>
  <p>Presidential ballot on June 9 and 16, 2027. Central stake: mandate for institutional renewal and ecological transition.</p>
</div>

<div class="led-timeline-item led-timeline--action">
  <time>July 2027</time>
  <h3>First digital referendums</h3>
  <p>Citizen consultation on convening the Sixth Republic and returning to the seven-year presidential term.</p>
</div>

<div class="led-timeline-item led-timeline--action">
  <time>September 2027</time>
  <h3>National energy sobriety plan</h3>
  <p>Launch of thermal insulation, rail deployment and air transport reduction programs.</p>
</div>

<div class="led-timeline-item led-timeline--action">
  <time>November 2027</time>
  <h3>Debt reduction trajectory</h3>
  <p>Adoption of the twenty-year framework law: spending cuts, tax reform and zero public debt objective.</p>
</div>

<div class="led-timeline-item led-timeline--action">
  <time>January 2028</time>
  <h3>Education reform — pilot phase</h3>
  <p>Trial of the new middle school format (4, 5 or 6 years) and A-level style baccalaureate in thirty schools.</p>
</div>

<div class="led-timeline-item led-timeline--action">
  <time>March 2028</time>
  <h3>Digital sovereignty</h3>
  <p>Launch of public competitions for French alternatives to major platforms and recovery of strategic mapping data.</p>
</div>

<div class="led-timeline-item led-timeline--action">
  <time>June 2028</time>
  <h3>Great Silver Dome — first session</h3>
  <p>Inaugural meeting in Lyon of the "Eternals" to set the first international ethical limits (AI, space, procreation).</p>
</div>

</div>
