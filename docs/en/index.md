# LED — Liberty, Environment, Democracy

<div class="led-hero" markdown="1">

**LED Party** — Program draft for the **2027 French presidential election**.

Three fundamental pillars for a sovereign, sustainable and democratic France.

</div>

<div class="led-pillars" markdown="1">

<div class="led-pillar" markdown="1">

### Liberty

Economic liberty within sustainable constraints (ESG), clear ethical limits and industrial sovereignty.

</div>

<div class="led-pillar" markdown="1">

### Environment

Demanding ecological policy, reduced energy consumption, territorial planning and organic agriculture.

</div>

<div class="led-pillar" markdown="1">

### Democracy

Sixth Republic, digital referendums, education reform and institutional renewal.

</div>
